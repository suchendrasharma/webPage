How it works?

Whale is a complete ordering system

  1. Integrate the ordering platform to web or mobile

  2. Servicemen defines availability through a web or mobile calendar.

  3. Customer defines service needs and choose a service time

  4. Order is sent through email or through mobile app to servicemen

  5. Servicemen performs service and customer is informed through email or mobile.
